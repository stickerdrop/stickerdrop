GOT QUESTIONS?

What file formats do you accept?
PNG, JPG, SVG, and PDF. For best results use 300 DPI or higher. Vector files (SVG, PDF) give the sharpest cut lines.
How long does shipping take?
Production takes 3-5 business days. Standard shipping adds 2-5 days. Free shipping on orders over $80, flat $15 under $80.
What is your minimum order?
No minimums. Order 1 or order 10,000 — we treat every order the same.
Can I get a proof before printing?
Digital proofs are available on orders over 250 units. Email us after ordering and we'll send one within 24 hours.
What is your return policy?
If there's a quality issue with your order we'll reprint or refund — no questions asked. Contact us within 7 days of delivery.
